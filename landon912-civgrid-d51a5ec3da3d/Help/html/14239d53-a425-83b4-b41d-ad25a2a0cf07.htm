Fields
  NameDescription
cameraAngle
The angle of the camera in degrees. Default is 65*.

The height of the camera from the terrain.

The speed at which the camera moves in units per second.
enableWrapping
Should the terrain wrap around when a camera moves too far off in the horizontal side? This adds a small overhead from running two cameras and a depth buffer.
